About Tigram
“Tigram” was created to facilitate timber tracing facility.The process starts with the applicant registering through the web application.Once registered,the applicant gets access to utilize the online portal. In order to file an application for timber transit, applicant has to enter all details as per Form 1 along with the permission received from revenue department, destination details and vehicle details to begin with the transit pass issuing process. All these documents and form are mandatory except for the vehicle details which can even be updated later. The application and all supported documents will be forwarded first to the revenue department for verification. On approval, these documents will further be passed on to the forest department for validation and assertion.
The transit request will be reviewed by Deputy Range Forest Officer and Range Forest Officer for its completeness along with physical inspection, then a transit pass will be issued which can be utilized by the applicant. To ensure secure and legal transit, a limit of 7 days has been allotted to the applicant for the transportation after which the pass becomes invalid. Also, to ensure that no delay or issue is caused for the applicant. A limit of 15 days is set within which the transit pass shall either be issued or rejected. Not adhering to which will lead to an automatic issuance of transit pass for the applicant. The status at each stage of inspection will be made available for the applicant by means of an application status page.
